# Pressure Cooker Short Ribs

## Ingredients

- 3 lb bone-in short ribs
- 2 in ginger
- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 onions
- 2 carrots
- salt & pepper
- 1.5 Tbsp sesame oil
- 2 Tbsp brown sugar
- 3 Tbsp sake (or other cooking alcohol)
- 6 Tbsp mirin
- 6 Tbsp soy sauce

## Instructions

Cut:

- onions into wedges
- carrots using "Rangiri" method
- ginger into large chunks

Throw into pot:

- garlic
- ginger
- short ribs
- carrot
- brown sugar
- sake
- mirin
- soy sauce
