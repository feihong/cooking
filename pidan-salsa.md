# Pidan Salsa

[Source](http://www.amandatastes.com/%E6%9B%BC%E9%A3%9F%E5%BF%AB%E8%AF%AD-%E7%9A%AE%E8%9B%8B%E8%8E%8E%E8%8E%8E%E9%85%B1/)

- 1 cucumber
- 2 tomatoes
- 2 green chilis
- 2 lime wedges
- 4 stalks cilantro
- 1 onion (maybe)
- 6 pidan
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- salt + pepper

Note: Remember to take out the seeds of the cucumbers, tomatoes, and chilis.
