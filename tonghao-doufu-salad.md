# Chrysanthemum greens & tofu salad

## Source

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S1cDorMOpZE&t=8s

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb tofu 
- 5 oz chrysanthemum greens
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp vinegar
- pinch of sugar
- pinch of white pepper
- 1 Tbsp peanut butter
- 1 Tbsp crushed peppers 
- 2 tsp sesame oil
